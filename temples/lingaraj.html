Lingaraj Temple

Lingaraj Temple, dating back to the 11th century, is the largest and one of the most revered temples in Bhubaneswar. It is a masterpiece of Kalinga architecture and a major pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva.

The temple complex is adorned with exquisite carvings, towering spires, and a sacred tank (Bindu Sagar). Non-Hindus are not allowed inside the main sanctum but can view the temple from a platform outside. The annual Shivaratri festival draws thousands of devotees.
