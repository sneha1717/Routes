Mukteshwar Temple

A 10th-century architectural marvel, Mukteshwar Temple is celebrated for its exquisite stone arch (torana), intricate carvings, and is a gem of Kalinga architecture. It is often called the 'Gem of Odisha'.

The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is a major pilgrimage site. Its unique blend of Buddhist, Jain, and Hindu elements makes it a must-visit for history and architecture lovers.
