Rajarani Temple

Rajarani Temple, built in the 11th century, is renowned for its stunning architecture and intricate sculptures. It is unique for its reddish-gold sandstone and absence of any presiding deity inside.

The temple is famous for its sculptural beauty, especially the figures of women and couples, and is a favorite among art historians. The annual Rajarani Music Festival is held here, celebrating classical Indian music.
