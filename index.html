About Khordha

Khordha district stands as the cultural and administrative heart of Odisha, home to the magnificent Jagannath Temple in Puri and the historic city of Bhubaneswar. This sacred land seamlessly blends ancient spirituality with modern development, offering visitors a unique journey through time.

From the architectural marvels of centuries-old temples to the vibrant festivals that color the streets, Khordha district represents the essence of Odishan culture. The district's strategic location makes it the perfect gateway to explore the rich heritage of Eastern India.

Whether you're a spiritual seeker, history enthusiast, or cultural explorer, Khordha offers experiences that will leave lasting impressions on your soul.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Classical Dance

Odissi, one of India's classical dance forms, originated in the temples of Odisha and tells stories through graceful movements and expressions.

Traditional Arts

Pattachitra paintings, stone carvings, and palm leaf manuscripts represent the rich artistic traditions of Khordha district.

Festivals

The world-famous Rath Yatra and numerous other festivals showcase the vibrant cultural calendar of the region.

Culinary Delights

From the famous Jagannath Temple prasad to traditional sweets like Rasgulla and Chhena Poda, taste the flavors of Odisha.
