Deras Dam

Deras Dam is a beautiful reservoir located about 15 km from Bhubaneswar, surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills. It is a popular picnic spot for families and nature lovers, offering boating, birdwatching, and serene views.

Constructed in the 1960s, Deras Dam is part of the Chandaka-Dampara Wildlife Sanctuary. The area is rich in flora and fauna, making it a great escape from city life. The dam is especially scenic during the monsoon and winter months.
