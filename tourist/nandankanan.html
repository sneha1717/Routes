Nandankanan Zoological Park

Nandankanan Zoological Park is a world-renowned zoo and botanical garden, famous for its white tigers, safaris, and rich biodiversity. It is a must-visit for families and wildlife enthusiasts.

Established in 1960, the park is spread over 400 hectares and includes a botanical garden, lake, and several animal enclosures. It is the first zoo in India to join the World Association of Zoos and Aquariums (WAZA).
